Scrumptious Cheesecake

What's in a cheesecake?

Do you know the saying 'what makes a good baked product?' is a whole lot of love, and especially lard. This cheesecake captures the essence of delighting your tastebuds while straining those heart muscles. Devour with caution...

Things you need:

  • 375g (3 8-packs) GRAHAM CRACKERS
  • 110g or 1c walnuts, toasted
  • 40g or 1/4c sugar
  • 3g or 2/3tsp salt
  • 30g or 1/4c flour
  • 170g (1.5 sticks) butter, melted

Make that Graham-Cracker crust

  1. Add ingredients except for butter to a food processor and spin until broken down into a sandy texture.
  2. Drizzle in butter while spinning. When ready, it should be able to hold itself together when you squeeze it. If it’s still crumbly, spin in a bit more butter.
  3. Spread into a greased 9” springform pan by pressing 50-60% of the graham cracker mixture evenly against the sides. See video @2:10 for method.
  4. Add remaining crumbs into the pan and press into an even layer on the bottom of the pan.
  5. Par bake at 350F/175C for 10 minutes
